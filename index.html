FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about StudyHub.

Is StudyHub free for students?

Yes! StudyHub is completely free during our public beta. We plan to keep a generous free tier for students even after full launch, with optional premium features for power users.

Can I access my notes on mobile?

Absolutely! StudyHub is fully responsive and works beautifully on any device. A dedicated mobile app is also in development for iOS and Android.

How does the AI Assistant work?

Our AI Assistant (coming soon) will analyze your notes, generate flashcards automatically, create practice quizzes, and provide personalized study recommendations based on your learning patterns.

Can I collaborate with classmates?

Yes! You can share notes, create group study rooms, and collaborate on mind maps in real-time. Perfect for group projects and study sessions with friends.

Is my data secure?

Your data security is our top priority. We use end-to-end encryption, secure cloud storage, and follow industry-standard security practices. Your notes and data are always private.

What formats can I export my notes in?

StudyHub supports exporting your notes in PDF, Markdown, Word (.docx), and plain text formats. You can also backup your entire workspace to cloud storage services.
